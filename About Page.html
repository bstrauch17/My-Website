About Me

I am a passionate mechanical engineering senior at the University of Maryland, graduating in May 2024. My coursework and extracurriculars have allowed me to gain experience in Machine Design, Additive Manufacturing, Heat Transfer, Mechatronics, Automotive Design, Aircraft Propulsion and Flight Dynamics. I am interested in careers in aerospace and automotive design, as well as propulsion, hypersonics, and additive manufacturing technologies. In my free time, I enjoy 3D printing various trinkets, designing RC planes, cooking, staying active and hanging out with my friends and family.
